# Kabupaten-Sidoarjo
Sidoarjo Regency is a regency in East Java, Indonesia. The regency has its capital in the Sidoarjo District and covers an area of approximately 719.34 km², making it the geographically smallest regency in the province. Nevertheless, Sidoarjo is densely populated, with an estimated 2,027,874 residents as of mid-2024.
